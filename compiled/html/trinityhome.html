History of Trinity College Dublin

Founded in 1592, Trinity College Dublin is Ireland’s oldest university and a beacon of academic excellence. Its iconic Long Room Library houses some of the world’s most treasured manuscripts, including the renowned Book of Kells. Situated in the heart of Dublin, Trinity seamlessly blends historic charm with modern innovation, offering a dynamic environment for learning and discovery.
