History of Trinity College Dublin

Trinity College Dublin, founded in 1592, is more than just Ireland’s oldest university—it’s a living piece of history. From the breathtaking Long Room Library to the world-famous Book of Kells, its campus is a treasure trove of knowledge and culture. Nestled in the heart of Dublin, Trinity has been a hub for brilliant minds for centuries, blending tradition with cutting-edge innovation to shape the future. Whether you’re walking its cobbled paths or diving into groundbreaking research, Trinity is where history and discovery come to life.
